Active Ingredients
Caffeine Anhydrous 80 mg
Enhances sustained attention and reaction time
Acts as an adenosine receptor antagonist, blocking fatigue signals in the brain. Peak cognitive effect occurs 30–60 min post-ingestion; sustained for 4–6 hours.
L-Theanine 40 mg
Promotes focused calm; synergizes with caffeine
Increases alpha-wave brain activity. Caffeine + L-theanine combination demonstrates superior attention-switching performance versus caffeine alone (Owen et al., 2008).
Vitamin B Complex (B6, B12) 100% DV
Supports neural energy metabolism
B6 facilitates neurotransmitter synthesis; B12 maintains myelin sheath integrity for efficient neuronal signal transmission.
